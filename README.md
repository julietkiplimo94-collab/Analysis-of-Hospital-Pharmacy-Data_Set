2. Does a higher number of patient visits always lead to higher pharmacy revenue?			
Yes. They are positively correlated.
The analysis shows that as the Pharmacy costs increases the Patient visits increased as well, and in moths the pharmacy costs were low the patient visits dropped in number.
3. Which departments generate the highest pharmacy costs?	
The emergency Department generated the Highest pharmacy cost consistently throughout the year, except in June when there was a drop in costs for the department.
 In total the Emergency department generated a total revenue of KSH 34,400.
	


	
4. Are there age groups that consume more medication than others?
Yes. 
The age group of (25 – 27) year olds, recorded the highest average pharmacy revenue with Ksh 2,300/= as the average cost.		
The second age group (79 – 82-year-olds), recorded the second highest average pharmacy revenue with Ksh 1562/= as the average cost.
